# PCTracker

**PCTracker** is an inventory and profit tracking app designed for **trading card vendors and sellers**. It helps sellers track inventory, calculate profits, and manage business expenses when selling at **card shows, conventions, or online marketplaces**.

---

## Features

### Inventory Management
Track cards in your inventory with important information such as:

- Card name
- Purchase price
- Sale price
- Quantity
- Inventory status (available or sold)

Quickly see what cards are still available and what has already been sold.

### Profit Tracking
PCTracker helps you understand your earnings by tracking:

- Purchase price
- Sale price
- Profit per item

This makes it easy to monitor profitability across your inventory.

### Expense Tracking
Track miscellaneous expenses related to your card business, including:

- Table fees
- Travel expenses
- Supplies
- Event costs

This allows vendors to calculate **true net profits** from events or online sales.

### CSV Import and Export
PCTracker supports **CSV data management**.

You can:

- Import existing inventory from spreadsheets
- Export inventory and sales data
- Back up your records
- Analyze data in Excel or Google Sheets

PCTracker is designed for:

- Trading card vendors at conventions and shows
- Pokémon TCG sellers

If you buy and sell collectible cards, PCTracker helps keep your **inventory and finances organized**.
